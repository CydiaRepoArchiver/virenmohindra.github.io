Our Story

Girls for Tomorrow (GFT) was born because of the mute eloquence of one girl who singlehandedly shook the entire nation to stand against the fight for women's right. As the Nirbhaya case became a household name for courage and valor in this nation, We felt inspired to contribute, in our own small way, to this change for tomorrow.
Read More
A girl today isn’t safe anywhere. In today’s male dominated society, there is very little scope for immediate change. We can keep saying that society needs to change, and hopefully, one day it will; but in order to make that happen, we must change ourselves. We must make sure that if we ever are attacked, we are prepared to protect ourselves because society isn’t ready to do that for us just as yet. In order to do so, a certain amount of disillusionment is required. Women today are vulnerable to assault irrespective of which strata of society they belong to. We all think that something so terrible can never happen to us. We need to hope for the best and yet, prepare for the worst. Until we accept that we need to be protected, we will never work towards it. We also need to learn that should anything happen, keeping quiet about it is not the way to go. We need to be very liberal. We need to stop treating the victims as felons and allowing the perpetrators to get away. All of this can only be done when we spread awareness. We make women and girls aware of their rights: their basic rights as humans and their rights against oppression. We need to teach them that it is imperative that they stand up for themselves. To fight against injustice and the people doing it. Girls For Tomorrow is the first step towards achieving all this. By teaching girls how to defend themselves in unfortunate situations, we are not only helping them come out of these situations, but also making a statement that we’re not going to sit quiet. There is a long way to go before the world is ideal, and there is a lot more to do. We have to change society, but we need to start with ourselves.

Girls For Tomorrow

Create awareness and inspire change through active participation of the youth to combat gender based violence in our country.

What do we do?

GFT helps neighbourhood girls in skill building whether its learning few martial arts techniques or building strong foundation through education or just doing simple yoga to help build strength within.

What will it include?

GFT believes in creating awareness against gender based violence through building networks and channels in our community, neighbourhood, city and acorss India as it involves change in thinking.

How will it work?

GFT is an organisation that helps in skill building with the help of instructors and co-founders. It includes mastery of martial arts skills as well as some simple yogic methods.

How will it be presented?

It will be presented in the for of workshops conducted on a weekly basis
